Library Week District Program Held at Our School

Library Week District Program was celebrated at our school. Our students participated in activities aimed at developing a love of books and reading habits. The program, which included participants from various schools in our district, was met with great interest.

Source: sehithamdibeyorta.meb.k12.tr · April 6, 2026

eTwinning Project

Once Upon … A Kingdom

We have completed the February activity for our "Once Upon… A Kingdom" eTwinning project. Our February activity focused on English idioms. We formed a total of 6 mixed national teams, with three teams representing different countries, and selected two students from each school to represent them. One school from each team selected idioms; the second school created a dialogue using the idiom; and the third school prepared a visual representation of the idiom. Our school was involved in the idiom selection process for the first team. At the end of the activity, our students both learned English idioms and created a fun booklet of idioms.

Source: sehithamdibeyorta.meb.k12.tr · March 9, 2026

Commemoration Ceremony

March 18 Gallipoli Victory and Martyrs Commemoration Day Observed

The 18th of March, the Commemoration Day of the Çanakkale Victory and Martyrs, was respectfully celebrated at our school with ceremonies and events. Our students and teachers gratefully remembered the martyrs who gave their lives in Çanakkale. The ceremony included poetry readings and presentations.

Source: sehithamdibeyorta.meb.k12.tr · March 31, 2026

eTwinning

eTwinning English Idioms Booklet

Our English idioms booklet, created in collaboration with our international partners as part of our eTwinning project, has been completed. Created with contributions from students from six different countries, the booklet has become both an educational and fun resource. Through this work, our students have improved their language skills and gained experience in collaborating with friends from different cultures.

Source: sehithamdibeyorta.meb.k12.tr · March 9, 2026
